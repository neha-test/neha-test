Contract Buyout Process
This process begins when a Specialist receives a call, follows call handling and authentication processes and determines customer reason for calling is to inquire about their Contract Buyout.
  • If customer needs assistance registering, Proceed to Step 1
  • If customer is questioning status of Contract Buyout, ensure all qualifications have been met and advise customer to check status on website (See Step 4) and provide toll free number 1-866-661-2103
 
STEPACTION
1.
Have the customer go to: http://twcbc.rewardpromo.com/ContractBuyout to activate the Contract Buyout payment.
 

NOTE: Advise the customer they will need their Redemption code to register
2.
Advise the customer to enter Redemption Code and click Submit.
 
The customer will be directed to the Registration page where they will need to fill in the required information outlined, and then click Next.

NOTE: If it has been more than 3 weeks since installation and they haven't received their Redemption code, have them click below.

3.
Fill in outlined fields and click Search
 
4.Once the customer is successfully registered they will be taken to the Status page where they will be informed their submission is being validated.

NOTE: If the customer has already submitted their registration, they can return at any time and check the progression of their status by logging into the website and clicking the STATUS tab.
  • The customer can check the status of their Contract Buyout payment by using one of the search options below. 



Contract Buyout Escalation Process
The process below should be used for repeat inquiries, or for when the customer has met all criteria, the allotted time has expired and the customer still has not received their Contract Buyout.
 
STEPACTION
1.
Send an email to: nikki.muranko@twcable.com 
  • Subject of email should read: “Contract Buyout Request Escalation”
NOTE: This Distribution list will only handle Contract Buyout escalations. Any other inquires (i.e. billing, sales, technical) should be handled by the appropriate department.
 
2.
Include the following information in the body of the email:  
  • Account Number
  • Redemption Code
  • Business Name
  • Email Address
  • Install Date
  • Service Address
3.
Advise the customer it may take up to 4-6 weeks for receipt of their payment.
